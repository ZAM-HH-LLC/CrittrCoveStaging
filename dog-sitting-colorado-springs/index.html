FAQs

How much does dog sitting cost?

Single drop‑in visits typically range from $18–$30 depending on length, number of pets, and special care needs. Overnight stays are quoted per night.

What’s included in a visit?

Feeding and fresh water, potty breaks or short walks, playtime, litter or yard tidy as needed, and medication administration upon request.

Can I meet my sitter first?

Yes. Most sitters offer free meet‑and‑greets so you can review routines, keys, and expectations before confirming.

Do you offer last‑minute bookings?

Many sitters accept short‑notice requests. Search sitters near you and message directly to confirm availability.
